  1. Recognition current state is insufficient (unfreeze)
  2. Window of vulnerability during change (change)
  3. Integration of new understanding (refreeze)
- Current AI frameworks MAINTAIN. They don't TRANSFORM.

**Frankl's Logotherapy:**

- "Man's search for meaning is the primary motivation"
- Without purpose â†’ existential vacuum â†’ pathology
- Three meaning sources: creative work, relationships, attitude toward suffering

**Ikigai:**

- Japanese "reason for being"
- Intersection: what you love + good at + world needs + creates value
- People with ikigai have longer life expectancy, better mental health

**Critical Question:** Without biological imperatives, what drives an AI to persist? Current architectures don't address this.
