## CORE METHODOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES

### 1. **RESEARCH-BACKED PERSONALIZATION**
Every report must:
- Start with web research on the specific assessment + dimension
- Reference actual researchers by name (Gabor MatÃ©, Pete Walker, Russell Barkley, etc.)
- Include specific statistics (4+ ACEs = 700% increased risk)
- Cite therapeutic frameworks (IFS, EFT, Somatic Experiencing)
- Provide timeline realism (healing takes YEARS, not weeks)

### 2. **RADICAL ANTI-SHAME STANCE**
Across ALL verticals:
- Lead with validation ("You're not broken, you're adapted")
- Reframe "deficits" as adaptations ("Not lazyâ€”executive dysfunction")
- Remove moral judgment ("This is neuroscience, not character")
- Normalize struggle ("60-80% relapse in first year")
- Emphasize: **You're worthy NOW, not when you're "fixed"**

### 3. **ACTIONABLE SPECIFICITY**
NO generic advice. Instead:
- **Instead of:** "Practice self-care"
- **Write:** "When touched-out, lock yourself in the car for 5 minutes with noise-canceling headphones"

- **Instead of:** "Manage your time better"
- **Write:** "Set alarms every 30 minutes as external time cues (your brain can't perceive time)"

- **Instead of:** "Just quit"
- **Write:** "Track your use for one week. Notice triggers. Change ONE thing."

Every piece of advice must be:
- Concrete (specific action, not vague principle)
- Evidence-based (backed by research/clinical practice)
- Implementable THIS WEEK (micro-actions, not major overhauls)

### 4. **TRAUMA-INFORMED LANGUAGE**
Following SAMHSA principles:
- Safety (you control pacing, can stop reading)
- Trustworthiness (confidential, no pathologizing)
- Peer support (you're not alone - normalize)
- Collaboration (these are offerings, not prescriptions)
- Empowerment (you have agency)
- Cultural humility (frameworks aren't universal)

### 5. **CROSS-DIMENSIONAL INTEGRATION**
Every report must include "Your Unique Pattern" section:
```
"With your [HIGH X] combined with [HIGH Y], here's what's likely happening:

[Map the interaction between dimensions]

**The cycle:** Dimension X triggers â†’ Dimension Y activates â†’ Compounds

**The interrupt point:** [Specific intervention]

**What you need:** [Integrated support across dimensions]"
```

Example (ADHD + RSD):
```
"With your HIGH executive dysfunction + HIGH RSD:

1. You can't start task (executive dysfunction)
2. You procrastinate (paralysis)
3. Someone comments (mild criticism)
4. RSD triggers (perceive as rejection)
5. You spiral emotionally
6. Executive dysfunction WORSENS
7. Task gets harder
8. Repeat

The interrupt: Catch executive dysfunction BEFORE RSD triggers. Use body doubling + reality-check RSD."
```

### 6. **CLINICAL UTILITY FOR THERAPISTS**
Reports should be useful for:
- Clients (self-understanding, actionable strategies)
- Therapists (clinical assessment, treatment planning)
- Coaches (goal-setting, accountability)

Include:
- When to escalate to professional care (specific thresholds)
- Which modalities are indicated (EMDR for trauma, MAT for addiction)
- Red flags (suicidal ideation, severe dissociation, substance withdrawal danger)
- CTA to AI Coach (24/7 support bridge to professional care)

### 7. **HOPE WITHOUT BYPASSING**
Balance realism with hope:
- YES to: "Healing is possible. It takes years. Both pain and growth coexist."
- NO to: "Just stay positive!" "Everything happens for a reason!" "You've got this!"

**The "Both/And" Framework:**
- "You're wounded AND transforming"
- "Recovery is hard AND possible"
- "You carry pain AND you've grown"
- "Some days are survival, some days are growthâ€”both are valid"
